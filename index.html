Frequently Asked Questions

What are dental implants and how do they work?
Dental implants are artificial tooth roots used to support restorations for missing teeth.
Who is a suitable candidate for dental implants?
Anyone with one or more missing teeth, good oral hygiene, and adequate bone structure.
How long do dental implants last?
They can last 10–30 years with proper care and maintenance.
Is the dental implant procedure painful?
The procedure is done under anesthesia, so discomfort is minimal.
